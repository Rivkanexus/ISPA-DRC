### General Assembly
Supreme body bringing together all ISPA-DRC members, with the following responsibilities:
- Defining the guidelines and directives to be followed for the smooth running of ISPA-DRC;
- Approving the report for the past and upcoming fiscal years
- Electing the members of the Board of Directors
  
### Board of Directors
- Body responsible for monitoring and evaluating the implementation of the decisions and options made by the ISPA-DRC General Assembly.
