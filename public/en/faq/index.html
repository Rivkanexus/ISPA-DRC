Frequently Asked Questions

Here, you will find answers to our most commonly asked questions.

Conditions for Membership in ISPA-DRC

What are the Conditions for Membership in ISPA-DRC?
Have a license attesting to the authorization to practice in the telecoms sector in the Democratic Republic of Congo and subscribe to the statutes and internal regulations by filling out the form - Application Form
How many types of members does ISPA-DRC have?
ISPA-DRC has three types of members;
- Joining member
- Full member
- Honorary member

Connecting to ISPA-DRC IXPs

What are the Conditions for Connecting to ISPA-DRC IXPs?
Have an ASN, and
- Subscribe to the RDC-IX charter by completing the form - Connection Agreement

Benefits of ISPA-DRC Activities

What do ISPA-RDC activities contribute to?
The activities of ISPA-DRC contribute to;
- The launch of projects aimed at improving the quality of Internet services;
- Advocacy leading to the adaptation of regulations and laws governing information and communication technologies, to technological developments;
- Upgrading managerial, technical and technological knowledge;
In addition to these areas, ISPA-RDC answers questions relating to the management of specific issues or exceptional projects in the Internet field.
