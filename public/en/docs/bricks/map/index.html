Map

Say you want to achieve a neat Map ‘component’ (see the first content section at the home page of this sample website). In order to get such a component, just use the markup below. The content in the brick starts with a screenshot of the given map area and the URL of the mapping service provider (Google, OpenstreetMaps, etc). They will automatically be converted into a clickable map. If you want to see this brick in action, take a look at the footer of the contact page.
